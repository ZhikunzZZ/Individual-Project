Welcome to FeedbackMate!

Here are some tips to help you get started:

Join a feedback channel

A feedback channel represents a course. If you want to join a feedback channel, click on the '+' button in the top left corner. Then, when jumping to the join feedback channel page, enter the pin code (provided by your teacher), you will be added to the designated feedback channel.

Access designated Feedback Channel page

When clicking on the designated channel icon on the left or the channel card below, you will enter the designated feedback channel.

Provide Feedback

When you enter a feedback channel, you can choose to enter a section in the section list on the left hand side. A section usually represents a lecture, a coursework, a quiz, etc. After entering the section, according to the guidelines, you can provide feedback comment, rate for section, and like comments in different blocks.

Update Profile

When hovering over the user's avatar in the top right corner and clicking on the edit profile button, you can go to the update profile page.
